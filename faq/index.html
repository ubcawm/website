Frequently Asked Questions

Everyone is welcome! AWM @ UBC is open to all UBC students — undergraduate and graduate — regardless of gender, program, or year. Our events and programs are designed to be inclusive and supportive of all who share an interest in mathematics.

No — joining AWM @ UBC is completely free. General membership and most events are free to attend.

We aim to run at least one event per month throughout the academic year (September to April). Check the Events page for what's coming up.

Our mentorship program pairs junior students (mentees) with senior students or professionals (mentors) based on shared interests, career goals, and availability. Pairs meet regularly throughout the semester. We run the program once per academic year. More details are on the Mentorship page.

We recruit new executive team members at the start of each academic year, usually in September. Positions include roles in events, communications, mentorship, outreach, and more. Keep an eye on our Instagram for applications when they open.
